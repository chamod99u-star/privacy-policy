Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Facebook application ("the App") handles information when you use it to automate motivational posts on Facebook. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Our application is designed to automate the posting of motivational content to Facebook pages. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Facebook Page Access: We require access to post content to your Facebook page. This access is granted through Facebook's OAuth system and is stored securely.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect basic usage statistics to improve the app's functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Posting motivational content to your Facebook page as requested
  • Maintaining and improving the app's functionality
  • Ensuring the security and proper operation of the service

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Access tokens are stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • We do not store your Facebook login credentials
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Access tokens are stored locally on your n8n instance and are not shared with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Used to generate motivational content. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data sent to their service. We do not send your personal information to OpenAI.
  • Facebook Graph API: Used to post content to your Facebook page. Facebook's privacy policy applies to interactions with their API.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app access at any time through Facebook settings
  • Stop using the app at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary for the app to function. When you revoke access through Facebook, all associated data is immediately removed.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
